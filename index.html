01. Me, Myself and I

Hello! My name is Alex and I'm a curiosity-driven Junior Front-End Developer who enjoy creating thing that live on the internet. I'm passionate about bringing both the technical and visual aspects of digital products to life.

I'm a fast learner, constantly trying to improve my technical abilities while providing good and clean code at the same time. I'm happiest when I'm creating, learning, exploring and thinking about how to make things better.
